Stop internet censoring - save the internet from the Copyright Act
What is the Copyright Act?
A bill to amend the Copyright Act is discussed in Korean National Assembly. This bill aims to give Korea Copyright Protection Agency (KCOPA), a governmental body, a power to block access to foreign websites that are regarded as copyright infringing. The bill, passed the relevant committee in December 2017, is waiting for approval of the Judiciary Committee, the final hurdle before referred to the plenary session.
